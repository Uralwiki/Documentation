Protective Collar, or simply Collar, consists of buying 1 lot of underlying security, buying a put option, and shorting a call option with a higher strike price. Both options have the same underlying Equity and the same expiration date. While it is not a requirement, both options are usually OTM. This strategy is the combination of a protective put and a covered call, which serves as a hedge against large losses with a lower cost, but limits the gain.
